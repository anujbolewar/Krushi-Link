Design and generate a highly converting, premium landing page (Home Page) for 'KrushiLink' (AgroVault FPO), an advanced SaaS platform designed to connect Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) directly with global and domestic high-value buyers.

**Target Audience:**
1. FPO Directors & Rural Co-operative Leaders (Needs to feel empowering, accessible, and rooted in community trust).
2. Global/Domestic Agri-Business Buyers, Retailers, and Exporters (Needs to feel secure, compliant, modern, and highly professional).

**Emotional & Brand Goals:**
- **Trust & Reliability:** The page MUST convey institutional-grade security. Buyers need to trust the traceability of the crops; Farmers need to trust they will get paid fairly.
- **Premium & Modern (SaaS-like):** Move away from traditional "cluttered agri-websites." Use plenty of whitespace, deep greens, gold accents, soft shadows, and crisp typography (e.g., Mukta and Noto Sans).
- **Connection & Roots:** It shouldn't feel like a purely cold tech company. It must balance sophisticated tech with the human element of farming (think high-quality imagery of prosperous farms, handshakes, and modern logistics).

**Required Sections & Structure:**
1. **Hero Section (Above the Fold):**
   - A bold, emotive headline (e.g., "Empowering Farmers. Enriching Global Markets.")
   - A clear subheadline explaining the value proposition (Connecting FPOs with verified buyers through transparent pricing and end-to-end traceability).
   - Dual Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons: "Join as FPO" (Primary) and "Register as Buyer" (Secondary).
   - Background: A high-quality, subtly darkened image or video background showing modern farming/export logistics.

2. **Social Proof & Trust Bar:**
   - Logos of partner organizations, government bodies, or "Trusted by 10,000+ Farmers across Maharashtra."

3. **Core Features (The "Why Us" Section):**
   - Present as premium icon-cards using Phosphor Icons.
   - *Traceability:* Seed-to-shelf tracking via QR.
   - *Live Market Pricing:* AI-driven fair price matching.
   - *Cold Chain Integrity:* Temperature monitoring for zero spoilage.
   - *Instant Compliance:* Auto-generated export certificates.

4. **How It Works (Process Flow):**
   - A visually appealing 3-step or 4-step pipeline (e.g., Harvest -> Quality Check -> Bid/Negotiate -> Secure Export) demonstrating the ease of the platform.

5. **Testimonials / Impact (Emotional Connection):**
   - A quote from an FPO leader about increased profits, alongside a quote from an international buyer about quality assurance.

6. **Footer & Call to Action:**
   - A final strong push to sign up. Include links to "About Us", "Compliance Standards", "Privacy Policy", and "Contact Support".

**Technical Constraints:**
- Return ONLY valid HTML/CSS.
- Use inline CSS or a semantic `<style>` block.
- Make it fully mobile-responsive.
- Include Phosphor Icons (`<script src="https://unpkg.com/@phosphor-icons/web"></script>`).
- Use a color palette of Deep Greens (e.g., #1B5E20), Surface whites, text-grays, and Amber/Gold accents for CTAs.
